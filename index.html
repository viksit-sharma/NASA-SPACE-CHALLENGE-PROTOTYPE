Our Stories

Bringing NASA's data to life with interactive experiences.

"Where Is It Now?" Spacecraft Tracker

Instead of seeing space as distant, this tracker makes it feel immediate and alive. Watch in real-time as the ISS speeds over your city at 28,000 km/h. We transform raw telemetry data into a living map, showing you exactly where humanity's greatest explorers are right now.

Postcards from Mars

This project turns raw data into personal mementos from another planet. Choose a rover like Curiosity or Perseverance and a Martian day (Sol) to receive a unique "postcard." Suddenly, you're not just looking at photos—you're part of the adventure, receiving a personal memory from millions of kilometers away.

Your Cosmic Calendar

What did the universe look like on your birthday? This personal time machine connects your story to the story of the cosmos. Pick any date that's special to you, and we'll fetch NASA's "Astronomy Picture of the Day" from that moment, turning it into a beautiful cosmic postcard from your past.

Earth Watcher's Journal

See our planet as astronauts do—a glowing blue marble against the blackness. Using daily images from a satellite 1.5 million kilometers away, this project turns our world into a visual journal. Flip through the days to appreciate the simple, stunning beauty of our living home.
